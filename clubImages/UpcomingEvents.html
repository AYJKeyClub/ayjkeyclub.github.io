World Partnership Walk

We will be fundraising for the Aga Khan Foundation Canada (AKFC) up until the date of the walk-a-thon. This foundation collects donations to tackle poverty in third-world countries by improving access to a quality education and health, improving food security, and building strong communities. Registration details will be discussed in the near future.
