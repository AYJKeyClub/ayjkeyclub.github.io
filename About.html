About Key Club

~

Key Club is the largest international student-led organization, consisting of about 260,000 members in 5,000 clubs around the world. It provides its members with lots of volunteer opportunities to serve the school, the community, as well as other people in this world. Together, we strive to make this world a better place.

Key Club isn't just any regular service club. Since the club is entirely student-led, Key Clubbers plan and carry out creative service projects by themselves. Through this process, the Club members develop initiative and valuable leadership skills.

Of course, Key Club would not exist without Kiwanis International. Kiwanis International offers sponsorship and mentorship to Key Clubs around the world.

We are caring and competent servant leaders transforming communities worldwide.
